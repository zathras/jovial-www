Pimpin' for the TV

Next to Disneyland, there's a sports bar called the "ESPN SportsZone." It's pretty easy to get yourself on the TV if you want - they have a small studio where the do crowd shots for the local postgame show. That's what me and my friend Dog did one fine Sunday. The post-game show is hosted by Michael "Coop" Cooper.

 

This was during the 2006 playoffs, when it seemed possible that the Lakers might end up playing the Clippers in the next round. I decided to go with the "Clippers hat, Lakers jersey" schtick, which is easy for me since I don't really follow basketball all that closely. Coop saw me, and referred to me as "one confused individual" :-)

 

Sit in front and go crazy when the camera pans to you, and you'll get noticed.

 

Or even a closeup!

 

Maybe the director will even suggest you get into a mock Lakers vs. Clippers fight...

 

But it's all in good fun.

By the way, when we tried to leave in the middle, Coop ran off the stage and tackled me, preventing us from going. I think they like the "crazy sports fan" act!
